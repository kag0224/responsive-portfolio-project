Bowtie Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • Bowtie pasta
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Pesto
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  • Boil pasta in salted water and olive oil

  • Cut cherry tomatoes in half

  • Drain pasta after cooked then add pesto and mix well

  • Serve and top with cherry tomatoes, parsley and parmesan cheese

Turkey Meatball Recipe

Ingredients

  • Ground turkey
  • Breadcrumbs
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Instructions

  • Combine turkey, breadcrumbs, onions, eggs, milk, garlic, salt and pepper.

  • Bake for 15-20 minutes or until cooked throughout
